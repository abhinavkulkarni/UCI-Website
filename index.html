PNAS Chemistry Corpus Analysis

I am currently doing analysis of PNAS Chemistry corpus from over two decades. Our hypothesis is that research in Chemistry, unlike other physical sciences such as Physics and Mathematics, is essentially conducted in much smaller groups and scope of collaboration is limited, in most cases, to other people in same affiilated institutes.
